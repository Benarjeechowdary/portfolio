Project Overview

Built an interactive dashboard and analytics solution for Apple, Google, and Meta stock data (2017–2022). Utilized advanced EDA and time-series methods in Power BI to visualize prices, trading volumes, and key financial metrics.

Business Problem

  • Analyzed yearly open, close, high, low, and adjusted prices for market insights
  • Detected surges (2021 peak prices; 2020 volumes) for investment timing strategy
  • Compared performance and volatility, guiding stakeholders’ portfolio decisions
  • Visualized comparative metrics for business communication

Data Analysis Process

1. Data Acquisition & Cleaning

Aggregated, validated, and cleaned NASDAQ stock history for Apple, Google, Meta (2017–2022) to ensure analysis accuracy.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Examined price trends, volume patterns, and annual KPIs with Python and Power BI. Identified leader companies and unique market events.

3. Dashboard Development

Designed a Power BI dashboard for intuitive visualization of stock averages, highs/lows, and trading volume peaks.

4. Insights Generation

Delivered actionable findings and strategic recommendations for improved market decisions.
