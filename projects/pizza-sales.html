Project Overview

Built an end-to-end pizza sales dashboard and analytics solution using SQL and Power BI. Uncovered key metrics and trends in revenue, order volumes, and product performance to empower strategic business decisions.

Business Problem

  • Integrated and cleaned sales data from thousands of transactions to deliver unified analytics
  • Analyzed total revenue ($817,860), order patterns, pizza size/category performance, and seasonal trends
  • Identified sales drivers, under-performing categories, and peak order hours for targeted marketing and inventory optimization
  • Visualized results to drive effective stakeholder communication and business planning

Data Analysis Process

1. Data Acquisition & Cleaning

Imported pizza sales data, handled missing values, and standardized records for robust analysis.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Used SQL and Excel to extract KPIs: total revenue, average order value ($38), pizzas per order (2.32), top-selling pizzas, least-selling categories.

3. Power BI Dashboard

Developed interactive visuals for sales trends, category breakdowns, and product rankings.

4. Insights & Strategy

Provided actionable recommendations that improved inventory management, boosted revenue, and optimized marketing timeframe and product focus.
