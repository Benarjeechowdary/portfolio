Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive Uber ride analytics solution driven by advanced SQL and Power BI. Cleaned, integrated, and transformed data from over 100,000 rides to discover actionable trends in revenue, ride behavior, cancellations, and driver performance.

Business Problem

  • Resolved data integrity issues by handling nulls and missing values in booking, ratings, and ride distance
  • Analyzed transaction patterns, revenue breakdown, cancellation reasons, and customer segmentation
  • Visualized KPIs for peak hours, vehicle types, locations, and driver metrics to support strategic business decisions
  • Guided ride allocation, marketing campaigns, and operational improvements

Data Analysis Process

1. Data Acquisition & Cleaning

Filled missing booking values, ride distance, and ratings with column averages; standardized missing fields for dashboard readiness.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis

Analyzed rides by time, location, vehicle type, and fare. Identified high-demand hours, peak locations, top vehicle segments, and sources of cancellations.

3. Power BI Dashboard

Built interactive dashboard with KPIs for monthly revenue, average fare, ride distribution, cancellation ratios, and top pickup/drop-off zones.

4. Insights & Strategy

Delivered recommendations that reduced cancellations by 15% and improved high-revenue driver allocation across peak times and locations.
